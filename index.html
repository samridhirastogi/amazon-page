How to Talk to Your Cat About Gun Safety: And Abstinence, Drugs, Satanism, and Other Dangers That Threaten Their Nine Lives

by Samridhi Rastogi (Author)

Cats are the ultimate masterminds of mischief, wrapped in a fur coat of pure cuteness. They’ll stare at you like they’ve just discovered the meaning of life, only to knock over a glass of water just because it was standing there too confidently. These furry ninjas can leap across the room with the grace of an Olympic athlete, only to land in a box that’s clearly two sizes too small. And let’s not forget their uncanny ability to nap for 18 hours straight, only waking up at 3 AM to perform a solo concert, complete with interpretive dance moves across your face. Cats: because why have a pet when you can have a tiny, four-legged comedian who believes they’re in charge of the universe?
